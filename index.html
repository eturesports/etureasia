Academic Programs

Academic Programs · Educational offerings & Degrees

Visa-Compliant,
Fully Accredited Pathways

To ensure a seamless transition for international student-athletes, ETURE has established strategic partnerships with top-tier academic institutions, including Missouri State University. Through these collaborations, players are fully enrolled in certified, visa-compliant courses, ranging from intensive Spanish language programs to specialized Global Sports Performance certificates.

Under this framework, players enter Spain with official student visa status. This structure allows them to securely maintain their academic progress, preserve future NCAA eligibility, and obtain legitimate residency while training and competing in a professional European environment. Through our strategic institutional partnerships, ETURE offers visa-compliant, fully accredited academic pathways that cater to various educational levels. This ensures our student-athletes balance elite athletic development with official qualifications.

Designed for high school graduates looking to develop their game before college or a professional career, while earning university-level credits or coaching certifications.

Students can enroll in specialized higher education courses certified by the Universitat de Lleida, earning official European academic credits.

Coaching Certifications (GM Football Academy)

In partnership with one of Spain's premier coaching schools, players have the opportunity to study and earn official football coaching badges (Curso de Entrenador), opening up professional pathways within the sports industry.

Saba Verda International School (Tarragona)

For younger prospects, we partner with Saba Verda to provide a seamless, accredited international high school curriculum. This allows student-athletes to continue their secondary education in Spain without disrupting their academic progress back home.

Missouri State University Partnership

Through our official partnership with Missouri State University, students have the unique opportunity to enroll in flexible online or hybrid university classes. This structure is ideal for players looking to advance their American degree, maintain future NCAA eligibility, and train full-time in Europe.
